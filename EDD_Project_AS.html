Element A - Problem Identification and Justification

Problem Statement (Block 3B)

Dirty countertops can negatively impact hygiene, organization, preparation areas, and take up a lot of time to clean.

In a study conducted by the journal Mindfulness, clean surfaces were linked to a decrease in anxiety of about 27%.

In addition, a 2017 study by The National Library of Medicine emphasizes hygiene. In this study, S. Aureus — a bacteria which could lead to pneumonia and bloodstream infections — was found on countertops and refrigerator door handles in 39% of households.

Furthermore, according to Wallstone USA and Onix Cleaning Services, 30–45 minutes a day is recommended for deep cleaning countertops, but it could take longer with stubborn stains.

These three main issues could all be addressed through a solution that addresses the problem of dirty countertops.
